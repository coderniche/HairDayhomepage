Hair and Care

Hair Day is a boutique hair salon based in Madison, Wisconsin. We specialize in cut, color and styling. We are well known for our edgy hair colors and on-trend cuts but did you know we also offer makeup and nailcare?

Click here to see our gallery and see the amazing looks we have produced. Speak to our stylists if there is something in particular you would like.

Enjoy our warm and relaxing atmosphere which will leave you looking and feeling your best self.

Pria

Pria, the owner of Hair Day, started specializing in edgy custom coloring techniques as soon as she qualified as a colorist.

Pria and her good friend and talented hairdresser, Garry, opened the salon...

They now have two stylists, three colorists, one manicurist and one make-up artist.

Bookings

The salon can only accommodate a small number of coloring appointments each week, so book early to avoid disappointment.

If you have a special occasion coming up, we take bookings up to one year in advance.

Tutorials
